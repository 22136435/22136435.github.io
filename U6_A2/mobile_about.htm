About Celtec Games

Welcome to Celtec Games, an independent game development studio based in North East Wales. We are passionate developers, designers, and storytellers committed to creating unforgettable gaming experiences.

Celtec Games was founded in 2020 and is committed to creating challenging, immersive, and emotionally engaging games that push the boundaries of gameplay and narrative. Our flagship titles combine innovative gameplay mechanics with rich storytelling, often inspired by Welsh mythology, history, and landscapes.
